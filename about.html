About Witteklip Secondary School

Committed to education, growth, and opportunity.

School Overview

Witteklip Secondary School is a public secondary school situated at 3 Witteklip Street, Croftdene, Chatsworth, Durban, KwaZulu-Natal. The school serves approximately 400+ learners and is led by Principal Mr S Moodley.

The school is focused on creating a professional, disciplined, and supportive learning environment where learners can develop confidence, responsibility, and academic growth.

Our History

Founded on 25 July 1967, Witteklip Secondary School has built a strong legacy of service and community impact. Over the years, the school has continued to support generations of learners and families in Croftdene and surrounding areas.

Our Vision

“To develop disciplined, educated, and responsible citizens.”

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide quality education, encourage learner growth, and create opportunities that prepare learners for a better future.
